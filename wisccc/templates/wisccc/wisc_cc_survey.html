We're excited for this fourth season of our cover crop citizen science project, gathering real world data to better understand how cover crops are being used in Wisconsin. We're part of a collaborative effort of researchers, extension folks, and farmers like you working together to develop sound cover crop strategies. We're also building a long-term statewide database of cover crop performance helping improve decision support tools like SnapPlus, and developing better strategies for reducing erosion and conserving nutrients.

Thank you for filling out this survey. Please proceed ONLY if you have registered with us. (To register, send an email with your name, mailing address, phone number, and county to Mrill Ingram mingram@wisc.edu)
