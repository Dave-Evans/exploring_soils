How it works:

Agronomists/CCAs Needed! Earn a $50 honorarium participating in a cover crop focus group for agronomists and help test a new data visualization tool (checkout our map and graph).

Are you a certified crop advisor or agronomist? What on-farm cover crop data do you find most useful, and how would you prefer that information be shared? Eligible participants will be located in Wisconsin or another upper midwestern state and have at least some experience advising growers on cover crops.

Your input will help us develop a tool that shows how different practices and conditions affect cover crop performance, making sure the data is practical and relevant to your work with farmers.
