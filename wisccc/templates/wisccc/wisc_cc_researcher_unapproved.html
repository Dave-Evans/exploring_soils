Collaborating researchers

Hello, if you are interested in using our data, we welcome your collaboration. To get started, email Mrill Ingram (mingram@wisc.edu). She will begin the conversation about our data and how you might use it. You will need to fill out a data use agreement form and sign it. After this is done, you will be given an account to this website and given permissions to download the data. These permissions will expire after a year.

If you have already been given an account, and have signed in, and you are still seeing this page, please email Mrill Ingram (mingram@wisc.edu) to verify that permissions have been assigned appropriately. If permissions have, perhaps, been assigned recently, then try logging out and back in again.
