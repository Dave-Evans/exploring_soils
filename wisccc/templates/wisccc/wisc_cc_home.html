Welcome

Since 2020, farmers around Wisconsin have shared cover crop and other soil health practices with us. Each colored dot on the map represents one of their fields. Farmers completed a survey and collected cover crop samples in Fall 2020, 2021, 2022, or 2023.

Map of participating citizen science farmers.

Do you see your dot? Click each location for a popup box with cover crop types, seeding rate, fertility management biomass (how much plant grew), and more.
